Projects

Deep Learning for speech recognition

I took part in a hackathon, which was offered by a company manufacturing technological ovens. The project aimed at realizing an intelligent algorithm to manage an oven by the speech recognition. To achieve this, I collaborated with a team composed of four guys who had different backgrounds from mine: one was a business student and the others studied physics. IoT & Industry 4.0.

In May 2017 I could take part in a free European programme, whose aim was to provide us some knowledge about industrial automation. I learnt how to make use of Arduino and Raspberry Pi 3 with the usage of some sensors. In particular, this course aimed at teaching how to use sensors and microcontrollers to acquire some information, and then to elaborate it. All the participants and I built a simple temperature sensor feedback network to monitor the temperature of a room: once acquired it, it was sent by Wi-Fi to a Raspberry microcontroller to elaborate it and to save it in a MySQL database. The elaboration consisted in giving error if the temperature was below or above a certain value. At the end of the course, each of us had to do a real project for a factory, whose goal was to use sensors and microcontroller to monitor their production (e.g. counting the manufactured products, duration of the production, chronology of the manufactured products every day and so on).
